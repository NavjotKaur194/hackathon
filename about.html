About Codegenix

Shaping Tomorrow's Technology Leaders

Our History

Founded in 2015, Codegenix Institute of Technology emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving technology industry. Starting with just two departments and 100 students, we have grown into a premier institution with state-of-the-art facilities and over 1000 successful alumni working in leading tech companies worldwide.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that nurtures innovative minds and creates technology leaders who drive positive change in society through cutting-edge research and development.

Our Mission

  • Provide world-class education in technology and engineering
  • Foster innovation and research mindset among students
  • Develop industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values
  • Promote sustainable technological solutions for societal challenges
